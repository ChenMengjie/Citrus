Welcome to Citrus Pages.

Citrus is a toolkit for single cell sequencing analysis under development.

Available methods

Now a normalized method and a clustering method are available in the beta version. Tutorial for the usage will be available shortly.

Method 1: scPLS: a normalization method to remove unwanted variation using both control and target genes.

Why do we call it citrus?

Citrus is not a short name for any of our methods. To honor Bowtie/Cufflinks, Salmon/Sailfish series, we are creating a fruit collection.
